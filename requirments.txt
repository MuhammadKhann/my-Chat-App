

## 1. Authentication & Identity
* **Phone-based Onboarding:** Registration via OTP (SMS/WhatsApp/Voice).
* **Profile Customization:** Display name, bio, animated avatars, and "Status" (24-hour disappearing updates).

## 2. Core Messaging & Real-Time Sync
* **Text Messaging:** 1-on-1 and Group chats with markdown support (bold, italics, code).
* **Presence Indicators:** "Online," "Last Seen," and "Typing..." or "Recording..." status.
* **Threaded Replies:** Replying to specific messages to maintain conversation flow.
* **Message Editing/Deletion:** Edit sent messages (with history) or "Delete for Everyone."
* **Starred/Pinned Messages:** Save important info or pin chats to the top of the list.

## 3. Multimedia & File Handling
* **Voice :** Audio snippets recorded instantly.
* **Universal File Sharing:** Support for PDFs, ZIPs, APKs, etc., up to 100MB.
* **In-App Media Player:** Play videos or audio files (with 1.5x/2x speed) without leaving the chat.
* **Stickers & GIFs:** Integration with Giphy/Tenor and custom animated sticker packs.

## 4. Voice & Video Communication (VoIP)
* **HD Calling:** 1-on-1 crystal clear audio and video calls via WebRTC.
* **Group Calling:** Large-scale video conferences with up to 100+ participants.
* **Screen Sharing:** Ability to share your screen during mobile or desktop calls.
* **Background Blur/Filters:** Virtual backgrounds or AR filters during video calls.
* **Call Recording:** Server-side or local recording for meetings (with participant consent).

## 5. Security & Privacy (The "Hard" Requirements)
* **End-to-End Encryption (E2EE):** Signal Protocol implementation for all 1-on-1 and Group chats.
* **Secret Chats:** Self-destructing messages with a countdown timer and screenshot alerts.
* **Privacy Controls:** Blacklisting (Blocking), reporting users, and granular "Who can see my..." settings.
* **Disappearing Media:** "View Once" photos and videos that delete after being opened.

## 6. Advanced 2026 "Super App" Features
* **Live Location:** Real-time location tracking for a specific duration (15m, 1h, 8h) .

## 7. Business & Enterprise Tools
* **Verified Accounts:** Blue/Green checkmarks for authenticated businesses.
* **Chat Folders:** Organize chats into "Work," "Family," or "Unread" folders.
